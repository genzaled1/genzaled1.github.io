    Subtopic 1: iPhone / Context

    The iPhone is widely considered one of the most innovative creations of the 21st century. This paper explores the key ways in which Apple has influenced culture through this innovative device. The iPhone is a huge catalyst to the culture. It has transformed the way people communicate by enabling quick messaging, video calls, and social media.It doesn't just stop at communication, but its intuitive design and constant innovation have made it a cultural uproar in society. Through these impacts, the iPhone alone demonstrates how Apple has become a powerful catalyst for technological and cultural innovation all over the globe.

    Subtopic 2: Apple Watch

    Like the iPhone, the Apple Watch has become a major catalyst of innovation. Apple designed this device to expand communication and functionality without ever touching a phone. Beyond messaging and phone calls, it allows users to track fitness, monitor health, and receive notifications instantly on their wrist. This was huge for its time! The Apple Watch was created with the goal of meeting the needs of fitness health and individuals seeking convenience. Its innovative design demonstrates how Apple continues to shape technology and daily habits. Through these features, the Apple Watch proves that Apple is another company that is a catalyst to the culture of technology and innovation.

    Subtopic 3: Apple AirPods

    Apple was able to create an earphone that does more than just play music. Apple took into consideration people with hearing disabilities when creating these products. Apple's genius and innovative settings that come with the AirPods made it possible for people to understand their significance in the culture and in innovation. These products had shaped the culture by providing the foundation of being an innovative company. Apple keeps on expanding their device innovation and I am eager to find out more.
